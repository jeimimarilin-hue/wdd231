Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Project Specifications & Planning Blueprint

Prepared by: Jeimi Díaz Mariani

1. Site Purpose & Target Audience

Site Purpose: To provide a centralized digital hub that promotes economic growth, empowers local businesses, and connects the community with vital resources, weather insights, and commerce opportunities.

Target Audience: Local business owners looking for growth and networking, entrepreneurs seeking organizational support, residents interested in community events, and visitors looking to discover the region.

2. General Design Specifications

  • Validity & Semantics: The entire site will be built using modern, valid, and contemporary semantic HTML5 structural elements, clean layout-driven CSS3, and modern modular JavaScript.
  • Consistency & UX: Navigation systems, headers, and footers will remain uniform across all pages to guarantee intuitive ease of navigation and a positive user experience.
  • Responsiveness: A mobile-first design strategy ensures fluid adaptability for mobile, tablet, and widescreen desktop clients.

3. Site Map Architecture

As structured in image_a35c22.png, the core hierarchy of the site maps directly to the following structure:

  • Home (Main landing hub)
  • Discover (Community info & media showcase)
  • Business Directory (Organization & business lists)
  • Join (Membership benefits & validation application)
  • About (Planned/Secondary)
  • Podcast (Planned/Secondary)
  • Events (Planned/Secondary)

4. Required Page Specifications

📄 Home Page

Will feature attention-grabbing introductory information, an interactive community brief, localized real-time weather updates, clear calls to action (CTA) for enrollment, and highlighted business spotlight zones.

📄 Discover Page

Dedicated to regional locale history, current demographic statistics, and active community events. It will incorporate a responsive image montage to visually orient visitors and showcase interesting key landmarks.

📄 Directory Page

Will house the complete list of local businesses and organizations that belong to the chamber, structured to toggle smoothly between card/grid and list views.

📄 Join Page

Will details the corporate and individual benefits of joining the chamber. It will host a dynamic membership application form outlining three distinct subscription tiers:

  • Non-Profit: Standard listings, basic community board access, and essential trainings.
  • Silver: Enhanced directory indexing, dedicated social media work/spotlights, and monthly luncheon entry.
  • Gold: Premium spotlight positioning, display advertising, full event sponsorships, and advanced publication perks.
